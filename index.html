Executive Summary

WASTEPRENEURS CHAD is a socially-driven waste management startup based in N’Djamena, Chad, tackling environmental and socioeconomic challenges. Improper waste disposal leads to severe pollution and health risks, while youth unemployment exceeds 50% and women face limited income opportunities. We offer localized waste collection, recycling education, training programs, and blockchain-based payment systems for transparency and fair compensation.

Our goal is to raise $250,000 through a 45-day donation-based crowdfunding campaign to finance waste collection trucks, recycling equipment, workforce training, digital platforms, and community engagement initiatives. Donors will directly contribute to a cleaner environment and sustainable livelihoods.
